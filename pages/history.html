Club History

Over a century of passion, tradition, and football excellence

1913

Foundation

Racing de Santander was founded on February 23, 1913, making it one of Spain's oldest football clubs.

1928

La Liga Founding Member

Racing became one of the founding members of Spain's top division, La Liga, in 1928.

1931

First Copa del Rey Final

Racing reached their first Copa del Rey final, marking a significant milestone in the club's history.

1988

El Sardinero Stadium

The club moved to their current home, El Sardinero Stadium, with a capacity of 22,222 spectators.

2008

UEFA Cup Qualification

Racing qualified for the UEFA Cup (now Europa League) for the first time in their history.

2024

LaLiga2 Campaign

Currently competing in LaLiga2, Racing continues to build towards a return to the top flight.
